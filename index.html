James Coxhead

ASP.NET Web Developer

Hello there...

...and thank you for visiting my website. I'm James, and I'm an ASP.NET back-end developer based just outside of London, UK. I create high-quality content managed web applications using Umbraco. I've put together this website to showcase me and my work.

My passion for web development started in my early teenage years where I would develop small websites using Microsoft FrontPage - I've come a long way since! From there, I learnt PHP before setting on .NET. I graduated from Loughborough University in 2014 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

During my time at Loughborough I took a placement year with Wickedweb as a junior developer. I re-joined Wickedweb after graduating from Loughborough; unfortunately the company ran into financial difficulties and I found myself looking for a new role. I moved on to Avenue Digital (a small marketing agency in Kent). In March 2016 I took on a new role with MerchantCantos, a creative communications agency based in Holborn, London.

Other things I enjoy include rowing (I compete regularly), road cycling, and photography (some of my photos are up on Flickr).
